# NYC Jobs Market Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes New York City job postings to uncover hiring trends, salary distributions, and demand across agencies and job categories. The goal is to provide insights useful to job seekers, workforce planners, and policymakers.

## Key Questions
- Which job categories offer the highest salaries?
- How does hiring demand vary by agency?
- What factors are most predictive of salary levels?

## Methods
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Hypothesis testing
- Regression and classification models

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## Key Findings
- Salary levels vary significantly by job category and agency.
- Technical and managerial roles command higher median salaries.
- Certain agencies show consistent hiring patterns year over year.
